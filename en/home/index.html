Composer, Yasuhisa Tsugata

（1971–2010）

About this site

This website was established in memory of composer Yasuhisa Tsugata, who passed away in 2010, and in the hope that it will provide many people with an opportunity to experience his work.

With the cooperation of the bereaved families and related parties, we are organizing and publishing the materials.

We hope that this beautiful music, which is quiet yet passionate, will be enjoyed far and wide for a long time to come.
